Global Academic Term Transition

Updating the global term advances all eligible students (ENROLLED, ADMITTED, or ACTIVE) to the next semester/year. Unpaid balances from the closing term are rolled into FORWARDED_BALANCE on the ledger. Students with forwarded debt of ₱100 or above are blocked from new enlistment until Cashier posts payment — they are not left on the previous term. This action cannot be undone.
